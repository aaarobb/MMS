Who We Are

Math Made Simple

Math Made Simple is an organization dedicated to helping students find joy in problem-solving and tackling difficult math questions. We believe that while math can be tedious and requires patience, it becomes incredibly rewarding — and even fun — once students begin to understand and master it. Our goal is to guide students through that journey: building confidence, curiosity, and a love for learning through engaging and thoughtful instruction.

Beyond one-on-one tutoring, our mission extends globally. We run free math programs for refugee youth in Houston in partnership with Kindred Vanguard, virtually tutor students in the township of Makhanda, South Africa weekly, and fundraise to expand access to education in rural India through Pratham USA. Math, for us, is a vehicle for opportunity, for connection, and for change.

With over 300+ students and an excited team of instructors from Houston-area schools and top colleges, we are here to help your child feel ahead of the curve in math.
