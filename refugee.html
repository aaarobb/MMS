Greater Houston Area Refugee Youth Tutoring ↗

In partnership with Awarenet, we virtually tutor children in the township of Makhanda, South Africa every Monday morning. Makhanda is among the areas with the lowest levels of math proficiency in the world — a gap we are determined to help close.

Over time, we have built strong, lasting relationships with our students while watching them gradually improve their skills. We are currently preparing them for the South African Math Olympiad.

March 2025 · Travel Program

Our team traveled to Makhanda this March. In partnership with Awarenet, Rhodes University, and local churches, we continued our in-person math programs, participated in community service, and created a short documentary on the anti-apartheid movement — exploring the history of colonialism, the resistance of the Xhosa people, and the forces that shaped the broader struggle.

Rural
India Focus

In partnership with Pratham USA, we are raising funds to expand access to quality education for children in rural India.

Pratham is one of the largest and most respected education nonprofits in India, reaching millions of children each year with proven, low-cost learning programs. We support their mission by fundraising directly through our dedicated campaign link — every dollar goes straight to them.

We have currently raised upwards of $3,000 and are continuing to grow that number.
